About

Fantasy Premier League is a platform that is dedicated to all the avid cricket lovers wherein players can use their skills and expertise in cricket to assemble imaginary or virtual teams of real players and compete against one another.The competition between these virtual teams is purely based on the statistical performance of those players in actual games.The main underlying motive is to form the strongest team spending a limited amount of money.
